SEER: Recommendation System That Explains.




Welcome to the homepage of SEER!


SEER is a pilot application of study undertaken by Udgam Mehetre as part of MSc Computing Practicum at Dublin City University.

Supervised by Dr. Alexander O'Connor and Dr. Fred Hollowood, the study aims to understand the importance of explanations in recommendation systems.
